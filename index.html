The object of the game is to be the first to mark three squares in a row.
A randomly selected player begins by placing an X then the other player places
an O and play continues, alternating between the two players. The game ends when
there are three X's or three O's going up/down, left/right or diagonally across
the board or when there are no more empty spaces.
